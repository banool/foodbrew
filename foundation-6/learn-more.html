Every year two million Australians will experience hunger, but over the same period, we will also waste of 200 kg of food for everyone.

Every year $8 billion worth of food gets wasted.

Food For Thought is committed to facilitating connections between organisations and charities, giving organisations an opportunity to donate their leftover foods to help those less fortunate.

Organisations are invited to become Thinkers, and connect with their favourite charities and make an ongoing pledge to donate. We hope to hear from you soon.
